About Us

Welcome to the Idea Management Portal of MITE
At Mangalore Institute of Technology and Engineering (MITE), we believe in the power of innovation and collaboration. The Idea Management Portal is designed to be a platform where creativity meets opportunity. Our goal is to empower students to share their innovative ideas, receive constructive feedback from faculty, and work together to turn those ideas into reality.The Idea Management Portal is more than just a tool; it’s a step towards fostering a culture of innovation that benefits both students and the institution. By encouraging idea-sharing and teamwork, we aim to create a lasting impact on the growth and development of MITE.

Why This Portal?


The Idea Management Portal offers several features that make it a unique and valuable tool for students and faculty alike:
1. Centralized Idea Repository: All student ideas are stored in one place, making it easy to track and review them.
2. Efficient Feedback System: Faculty can provide constructive feedback directly through the portal, helping students refine their ideas.
3. Collaboration Opportunities: Students can form groups, share ideas, and work together across branches to develop innovative solutions.
4. Progress Tracking: Students can review their idea submissions, monitor updates, and implement changes based on feedback.
5. Innovation Showcase: Outstanding ideas can be highlighted and presented for recognition within the institution or beyond.
6. User-Friendly Design: The portal ensures smooth navigation, making it easy for users to submit, view, and interact with ideas.
7. Encourages Growth: By promoting innovation and teamwork, the portal helps students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.p>

FAQ - Idea Management Portal

How can students register on the portal? ▼
Students can register by filling out the registration form with their personal and academic details. An email verification process is required to complete the registration.
How can faculty register? ▼
Faculty members need to provide their institutional email ID and credentials for verification to register on the portal.
Can students submit ideas individually? ▼
Yes, students can submit their ideas individually. The portal allows submissions with or without team members.
How does team member validation work? ▼
Team members are validated through their student IDs and registered accounts. All members must accept the invitation to join a team before submission.
How are submission deadlines managed? ▼
Submission deadlines are displayed on the portal dashboard. Reminders are sent via email as the deadline approaches.
